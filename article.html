The Clitoris Is Not a Button

Most adults were never taught about the clitoris — or worse, misinformation has created frustration and unnecessary shame.

The full anatomy matters

The clitoris is a complex organ. Most of it is internal. Understanding this improves communication and pleasure.

Pleasure is not instant

Arousal requires comfort, safety, and time. Expecting immediate results leads to anxiety.

Key Takeaways

  • Pleasure is learned, not automatic
  • Knowledge improves intimacy
  • Shame-free education empowers everyone
